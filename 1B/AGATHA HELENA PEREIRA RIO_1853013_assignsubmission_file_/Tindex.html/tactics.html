RHYTHMIC-TACTIC GYMNASTICS

TO IMPROVE A GYMNASTY PERFORMANCE, THERE ARE SOME IMPORTANT POINTS THAT SHOULD BE CONSIDERED:

  • Understanding and mastering the different techniques is essential to avoid making mistakes that can be penalized in the final score;
  • Some elements are more important than others, but they can also be more difficult or risky and may not match the chosen choreography;
  • The gymnast should focus on the most difficult elements that are worth more points, but also require more effort and training;
  • The connecting elements should not be forgotten, as they are the ones that give rhythm and balance to the entire exercise, being fundamental for a good performance;
  • Some elements can be executed in an unclear way, which can later cause problems of interpretation on the part of the referees; it is necessary to interpret all the elements in a clear and simple way so that there are no doubts;
  • A very good presentation is not easy, but it is not impossible either, it just takes a lot of dedication, effort and training.
