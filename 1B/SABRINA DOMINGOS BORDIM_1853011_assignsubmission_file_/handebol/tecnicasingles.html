Main handball techniques

  • Grip
  • Reception
  • Pass
  • Pitch
  • Dribble
  • Feint

Grip

The Handball is one of the most basic Handball Techniques, it is the act of holding the handball ball with one hand. The correct handball grip is done by holding the ball in the palm of the hand and squeezing (pressing it) with the fingertips.

Reception

Reception is one of the simplest Handball Techniques, it is the act of receiving (receiving) the ball. The reception in Handball must always be done with both parallel and slightly concave hands (shell-shaped) and facing forward.

Pass

The Pass is one of the main Handball Techniques, the pass is the act of throwing the ball between the players of the same team.

Pitch

The Throw is also one of the main Handball Techniques, it is through the throw that the goals are scored in a Handball game. Throwing is the act of throwing the ball towards the goal (goal) of the opposing team.

Dribble

Dribbling is a basic Handball technique, it is the act of bouncing the ball with one hand without losing control of the ball. Dribbling can be done with the player stationary or moving. The main function of dribbling in Handball is a progression with ball possession.

Feint

Feint in Handball is performed when a player in possession of the ball threatens to make a move in a certain direction to deceive and unbalance the opponent's marking and perform the move in another direction.
